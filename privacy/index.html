Peptide Pro Research

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-26

Peptide Pro Research is an educational research reference app for source-linked peptide profiles, literature records, and private local notes.

Current v1.0 Data Practices

  • No login or account creation.
  • The app is free and may display ads when ad support is enabled.
  • Ads may be served by Google AdMob.
  • No cloud sync.
  • No sale of data.
  • No health data API, health sensor, location, camera, or microphone collection.
  • User-created notes stay on the device unless the user exports or shares them through operating-system tools.
  • External source links may open websites outside the app with their own privacy practices.

Advertising

When advertising is enabled, third-party advertising SDKs such as Google AdMob may collect or receive device identifiers, advertising identifiers where permitted, app interaction data, diagnostics, approximate location derived from IP address, and related technical data. This data may be used for advertising, fraud prevention, analytics, and service reliability.

Users may manage available ad/privacy choices through the app's Privacy Options entry where available. Availability can depend on region, device settings, and Google consent requirements.

Local Notes

The app may store private notes locally on the user's device. The current v1.0 build does not send those notes to a server.

Deletion

Deleting the app removes local app data from the device, unless the user previously exported or shared notes elsewhere.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell user-created notes. When ad support is enabled, Google AdMob and related Google services may receive data as described above to provide advertising and related services.
